Journey Through Syed's Lense

Welcome to my personal space. I will share some of my favourite momemts and places in Canada. All the photos and videos on this website are captured by me. This website is a part of my class project where I tried to implement the leanings throught the course. Hope you will enjoy it. Thanks!

Sunset Contemplation

I am a opacarophile, which simply means lover of sunsets. This is my favourite time of the day when I can contemplate about my existance in this universe. Espacially the twilight period reminds me of my duration in this living existance is smaller then I think in day to day life. Which brings me to the point that we need to learn to live in the moment, even if its not the best moment of our life.

I want to mention a part of a poem by Rainer Maria Rilke, Let everything happen to you. Beauty and terror. Just keep going. No feeling is final.

Lets go back to our topic. I have seen sunsets on different setups; over sea, lake, mountains, city skyline, vast green fields etc. But if I have to choose a location, I would choose Briannia Beach which is located on the Ottawa river. It might not be the best scenic location for sunsets but it has a great sentimental value for me. Here, I have experienced a few of the most intense connections with the nature, consiousness together with some of the closest people in my life. Moments like these make me feel greatefull to be alive. This was my favourite spot in Ottawa, where I stayed for 3 years after I landed in Canada. Wherever I may go in this life, whenever I enjoy a sunset, I do and will always remember those moments.

Roadtrip Through The Mountains

I love roadtrips with friends and family. But when the road is nothing but the scenic highway through the Canadaian Rockies, love is not the word to describe it. I have took this highway multiple times, on my way to Banff, Jasper and Kelowna. But, everytime it hits me with the same speachless, awestruck feeling which no word can do it justice. It's beyond my grasp to describe, one has to experience it. In all of my journeys I was a passenger, and I cannot imagine how overwhelming it will be when I will be the one driving through the mountains. Well, Something to look forward to!
