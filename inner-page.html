Favourite Destination

Calgary Stampede 2021

It was the summer of 2021. The world was still recovering from the corona virus pandemic. Finally the travel restrictions were lifted and i found my chance to visit my brother and friends in calgary. I went to the calgary stampede for the first time.

I was there on the last day, so I was lucky to withess the closing ceremony. It was one of the best fireworks show I have ever seen.
